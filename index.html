The CSS Checkup featuring Tesla Motors

The Model S

The Model S is Tesla's first ever fully electric vehicle. This car has gone through many changes and is considered to be the worlds quickest sedan to date. Modern improvements to the car has significantly improved it's performance and range since its initial release, with now boasting over 500+ miles per charge and an incredible acceleration of a 0-60 in less than 2 seconds


The Model 3

The Model 3 is Tesla's entry level sedan, but don't let that turn you down from it, this car packs a powerful punch at the performance package level. With over 300 miles per charge and a 0-60 in roughly 3 seconds, this car makes a worthy competator in the sedan market. With a reasonable starting price point of 35k, the Model 3 is not to be overlooked.


The Model X

The Model X is Tesla's first fully electric SUV that is loaded with features, performance boosts and incredible charge range. Aside from the famouse "self-driving" feature all Tesla's can be equipped with, the Model X has falcon wing stlye passenger doors that completely sets the SUV apart from the competition.


The Model Y

The Model Y is Tesla's entry level SUV that comes with similar specifications as the Model 3. This SUV doesn't come with all the bells and whistles that the Model X does, however, for the price, performance, and features this car come loaded with, it is certainly a great contender.



Tesla's Special Vehicles

The Cyber Truck and Roadster

The Cyber Truck and Roadster are Tesla's newest addition to the family. While the Cyber Truck has yet to hit production, the over design and performance has buyers waiting paitently. The Design is something out of a futuristic movie and the price point is even more attractive. Similar to the Cyber Truck, the Roadster has turned heads being the quickest car in the world. Some might say it's even the fastest car in the world. With a ridiculously clean design, the Roadster accelerates to 60 miles-per-our in just shy of 1.6 seconds. These models are very steep in price, but for a valid reason.
